Recipe #1

  • cook: 10min
  • prep: 15min
  • Total Time: 25min
  • Serves: 3
  • calories: 477 kcal
  • Course: dinner, lunch
  • cuisine: japanese

    • How to Make

Ingredients Sauce
1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs
1 tbsp cornstarch 1 tbsp vegetable
1/2 stalk green onion finely sliced
1/2 tsp sesame seeds		 1/4 cup Sake
1/4 cup regular soy sauce
1 1/2 tbsp white granulated suger
1/4 cup mirin
2 tsp garlic minced
1 tsp ginger grated

Steps

Step 1: Slice chicken thighs into equal thirds.
Step 2: Combine sauce ingredients in a small saucepan and mix well.
Step 3: Bring to a boil so it's bubbling and let it boil forr 2 minutes to evaporate alcohol.
Step 4: Reamove heat and set aside.
Step 5: Using a brush, lightly dust sliced chicken with cornstarch on all sides with a dabbing motion. DO NOT COAT IN CORNSTARCH!
Step 6: Over medium high heat, add cooking oil into hot pan. Fry chicken thighs on both sides until browned. About 3-5 minutes each side.
Step 7: Pour sauce over top. Let it bubble to thicken and reduce, about 1-3 minutes. Flip chicken over to simmer on the other side for 30 seconds. Optional: garnish with sesame seeds and green onions. Enjoy!
